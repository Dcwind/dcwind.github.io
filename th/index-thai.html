History

.

  • 1986　December Japanese education begins from 1986.
  • 1990　April Certification is completed through Association for Promotion of Japanese Language Education.
  • 2002　April The title is changed to 「JSL Nihon Academy」.
  • 2005　April Office is established in Yangon city, Myanmar.
  • 2005　October The current school Facilities and grounds are acquired in Urasoe City, Okinawa prefecture.
  • 2008　April The International Business & Tourism College 「JSL International Academy」is founded.

Future planned establishment of local offices in Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam, Taiwan, South Korea, the State of Hawaii(U.S.A.), and Australia.
