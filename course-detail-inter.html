2. Admission Requirements / Qualification

Applicants must qualify all of the items below

  • 1. The successful completion of the basic 12 years or longer of school education in his/her home country. Or person who has a qualification to enter university in his/her home country. (Who resides in Japan)
  • 2. The completion of at least 6 months of Japanese language education at an institute recognized by the Association for the Promotion of Japanese Language Education. A period of 1 year may be required according to the institution.
  • 3. To possess adequate financial funds or be under the receivership of a scholarship.
  • 4. To engage in study and in the case of part time employment, the student is required to abide by the rules set down by the immigration authorities.

3. Required Application Documents

  • 1. Enrollment application form.( 1 photograph 4cm×3cm)
  • 2. Certificate of graduation and academic records from the final educational institution that the applicant has graduated from in their country of residence.
  • 3. Certificate of graduation, record of attendance, academic results from Japanese language school is required.※In accordance with regulations a student presently attending special courses such as university affiliated courses i.e. Those other than Japanese language schools are required to provide data to indicate current accreditation.
  • 4. The presentation of alien registration card and passport.
  • 5. Part-time employment permission certificate.(Shikakugai.katsudou.kyokasyo)
  • 6. Certification of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test or Score Certification of the Examination of Japanese University Admission for International Students.
  • 7. Letter of Pledge
  • 8. Record of financial status(i.e. Bank account details, cash transfer records)
  • 9. Medical certificate
  • 10. Selection fee《 20,000 yen》

4. Period of Application

  • For Entrance in April           October 01 ～ January 31 From Monday to Friday between the hours 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
  • For Entrance in October     April 01 ～ July 31 From Monday to Friday between the hours 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM

5. Selection

Selection process is based on the presented documents, an internal Japanese exam (equivalent of Japanese Language Proficiency Test-Grade 2) and an interview

6. Application Result

The applicant shall be notified of the results within 5 days after the acceptance of the application

7. Enrollment

The applicant is required to pay the initial tuition fee within a specified period. Upon completion of the enrollment procedure the applicants visa status must be altered prior to the final enrollment. In special cases a school representative can present the documents to the authorities.

9. University Transfer

Foreign students can have academic counseling. Those who have a goal in advancing to university, junior college or other institution can obtain detailed career guidance. Students, who have been in attendance for a minimum of two years, are able to a transfer to a university.
